Week Calendar is a week-focused Android calendar app that shows a clean, scrollable week view.

There is support for the following sync apps or solutions*:
- CalDAV
- Google Calendar
- Samsung Calendar
- Outlook / Exchange / Office365
- DAVx⁵

* The app will detect if there is a provider of calendar data if the **calendar permission** is granted.

Optionally you can turn on the setting to show the current ISO week number in the app's icon.
